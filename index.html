About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST) with a strong foundation in applied research and software engineering. Driven by a deep fascination with Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity, I have built my career at the intersection of rigorous academic inquiry and real-world problem solving — producing four peer-reviewed publications along the way.

My research portfolio — published at IEEE international conferences and indexed in IEEE Xplore and SSRN — spans reinforcement learning for low-light image enhancement, NLP-driven analysis of Bangla news corpora, deep learning for music genre classification, and soft computing applications. Each project reflects my commitment to building AI systems that are both technically sound and practically meaningful.

As a Software QA Engineer at Shikho, I translate research instincts into engineering discipline — architecting automated test frameworks with Playwright, validating complex REST and GraphQL APIs, and partnering with cross-functional teams to ship reliable software at scale. This dual lens — researcher and engineer — is what I bring to every problem I tackle.

PhD Research Vision

I aim to advance the frontier of trustworthy AI and cybersecurity — investigating adversarial robustness, privacy-preserving machine learning, and secure intelligent systems in collaboration with world-class research labs.
